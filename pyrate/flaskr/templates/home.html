The home advantage is expressed in terms of the game outcome measure, which is not necesarily points.

R2 measures how well the rating model fits the game outcome measure. It can range between 0 and 1, where 0 indicates that the outcomes are unrelated to the ratings, and 1 indicates that the ratings completely explain the outcomes.

"Consistency" measures the fraction of past game results (win or loss) that are correctly predicted by the ratings.
